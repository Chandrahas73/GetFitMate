Nutrition Suggestions

Discover personalized meal plans and tips for a healthier lifestyle.

Muscle Gain Nutrition

Consume 15-17 calories per pound for lean muscle growth. Eat 1.6-2.2g/kg protein from chicken, fish, eggs, and dairy. Include carbs like rice and oats for energy, and healthy fats from nuts and avocado. Pre-workout: protein + carbs. Post-workout: fast-digesting protein + carbs.

Fat Loss Nutrition

Maintain 12-14 calories per pound for fat loss. High protein (2-2.5g/kg) preserves muscle. Prioritize fiber-rich carbs and healthy fats while avoiding processed foods. Stay hydrated for better metabolism.

General Wellness

For overall fitness, consume 14-16 calories per pound. Maintain a balanced diet: 40% carbs, 30% protein, 30% fats. Drink 3-4L water daily and get enough vitamins and minerals for optimal performance.
